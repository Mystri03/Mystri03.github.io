05 — Research

Published Papers

Impact Factor: 8.586 IJSREM · September 2025 Peer-Reviewed · Open Access
Electricity Consumption Forecasting Using Time Series Analysis
International Journal of Scientific Research in Engineering & Management (IJSREM)
Volume 09, Issue 09 · ISSN: 2582-3930 · Indexed in Major Databases

Forecasts household electricity demand using ARIMA, SARIMA, SARIMAX and LSTM on the UCI dataset (2M+ records). LSTM achieved the lowest MAPE of 27.95% for long-term forecasting. SARIMAX incorporating temperature, weekend and holiday variables achieved MAE of 365.53 — a significant improvement over baseline statistical models. Demonstrates that hybrid deep learning approaches outperform statistical methods for complex long-term energy demand prediction.

Analysing the Impact of Discounts and Promotions using Data Analytics and Machine Learning
International Journal of Advanced Research in Science, Communication and Technology (IJARSCT)
Volume 5, Issue 2 · ISSN: 2581-9429 · Crossref Indexed

Investigates how promotional strategies influence consumer purchasing decisions using ML classification and regression. Analysed 250 survey responses across 14 features. Support Vector Classifier achieved 98.7% accuracy; Random Forest Classifier achieved 94.7%. Linear Regression, Random Forest and Gradient Boosting achieved R² = 1.000 in predicting promotional effectiveness — providing actionable insights for retail marketing strategy optimisation.
